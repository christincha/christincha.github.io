Self-Introduction

I am now a research assistant at Central for Neural Basis of Cognition, Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). I graduated from Electrical and Computer Engineering deparment at CMU. Before that I owned my Bachelor degree of Biomedical Engineering at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

My primary interest is how the brain functions to form perception and build model to explain it. In terms of method, I prefer dynamic system, deep learning, reinforcement learning and so on. So specifically, I would like to work in the field of Computational Neuroscience or Brain Computer Interface as well as Machine Learning.

Now I am working with Prof. Tai Sing Lee at Center for Neural basis of Cognition to study neural representation changes caused by training a set of stimuli. From 2016 to 2017 I worked with Prof. Xiaoying Tang investigating Brain Connectivity deficit induced by Alzheimer Disease. During my undergraduate study, I was under supervision of Prof. Sen Song at IDG/Mcgovern Institute, Tsinghua University. Besides, I worked with Prof. Xiaoquan Yang at Huazhong University of Science and Technology to design near-infrared light based wearable breast cancer detector.
